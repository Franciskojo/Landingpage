About Us

At IshmaelArts, we're passionate about transforming raw materials into stunning works of art. From the intricate details of pencil artwork to the functional beauty of woodworks, and the creative reuse of waste materials, we're dedicated to crafting unique pieces that inspire and delight. Explore our website to discover the beauty of handmade artistry.

About the Innovator

This 18-35-year-old male entrepreneur from Gomoa Central in the Central Region is addressing waste management challenges faced by communities, waste management authorities, and environmental organizations. His solution, the "Water Fountain" system, utilizes materials such as paper, boxes, and cement papers to create an innovative, efficient, and sustainable method for managing waste.

His expectations for the program are to receive support and guidance to refine his system, connect with other innovators, gain access to resources, and improve his knowledge in sustainable waste management. Ultimately, he seeks to make a significant positive impact on waste management and sustainability.
