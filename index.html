About Us

At IshmaelArts, creativity meets sustainability to bring art to life. We specialize in transforming raw materials into breathtaking masterpieces, blending craftsmanship with innovation. From the delicate precision of pencil artwork to the functional beauty of handcrafted woodworks, and the imaginative reuse of waste materials, every creation is a testament to our passion for art and sustainability.
Our mission is to inspire and delight through unique, handmade pieces that showcase the beauty of transformation. By reimagining discarded materials, we not only craft stunning works of art but also promote environmental consciousness. Explore our collection and experience the magic of artistry with a purpose. At IshmaelArts, every piece tells a story one of creativity, sustainability, and boundless imagination.

About the Innovator

This 18-35-year-old male entrepreneur from Gomoa West in the Central Region is addressing waste management challenges faced by communities, waste management authorities, and environmental organizations. His solution, the "Water Fountain" system, utilizes materials such as paper, boxes, and cement papers to create an innovative, efficient, and sustainable method for managing waste.

His expectations for the program are to receive support and guidance to refine his system, connect with other innovators, gain access to resources, and improve his knowledge in sustainable waste management. Ultimately, he seeks to make a significant positive impact on waste management and sustainability.
